About

I’m a PhD researcher in sociology (Freie Universität Berlin), studying how states pursue welfare through land politics, such as agrarian credit, land reform, rural infrastructure, and price supports, and how these interact with labour-based welfare and party competition. I build large-scale historical-comparative (1850–2025) datasets to model these historical-political relations.
